In 2009 two brothers named Ian and Neill Briggs founded the Briggs Automotive Company (BAC).

Ian and Neill briggs

Before making any cars, the brothers consulted multiple companies on design and engineering
projects before deciding to make it comlpletly on their own.

The result of their work was very lightweight, single seater, open cockpit, street legal racecar.

Photo of the BAC Mono

The first Mono was made in 2011, and its first sighting was at an auto show in Stuttgart, Germany

the first generation of Monos origionaly had a 2.3L four cylinder, heavily modified by Cosworth.
the engine made 280 hp at 7,700rpm

Even though the engine only made around 280 hp, the total weight of the Mono was almost half a ton!
This enabled the car to launch itself from 0-100kph in under 3 seconds!

In 2019, BAC unveiled the Mono R. It is a higher performance version of the standard Mono.
The Mono R had 38 more hp and weighed 25 kg less.
It also increased the size of the engine to 2.5L, to allow it to rev higher and output more torque.

In 2022, Adam Christodoulou performed a lap time on the Red Bull Ring of 1: 32.96, which is
four seconds faster than the Porsche 911 GT3 RS
